Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my health data stored? ▾
No. DiagnoX does not store or transmit any of your input data. All inputs are processed in-memory and discarded.
How accurate are the predictions? ▾
The Symptom Checker achieves ~92% accuracy, while SVM models for chronic conditions range between 78–85%.
Can I use this instead of a doctor? ▾
No. DiagnoX is for informational purposes ONLY. Always consult a qualified healthcare provider for clinical diagnosis.
